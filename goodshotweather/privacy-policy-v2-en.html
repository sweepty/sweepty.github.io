Privacy Policy

GoodShot Weather processes only the information reasonably necessary to provide weather, schedules, alerts, sign-in, and subscription features.

Version v2 Effective March 20, 2026
This page is formatted for in-app webview use. It may be updated when features materially change or when legal and platform requirements need to be reflected.

1. Information We Process

  • Account information: user identifier, sign-in provider, email address, or name provided through Firebase Authentication
  • Service data: rounding schedules, favorite golf courses, notification preferences, and related in-app data
  • Device and notification data: FCM token, platform, and app version used for device registration and push notification delivery
  • Subscription data: store purchase status and RevenueCat-linked data used to verify, restore, and maintain Premium access
  • Ads and analytics data: advertising consent state and anonymized or pseudonymized event data used for service improvement and ad operations

2. Why We Process It

  • To keep users signed in and sync data across devices
  • To manage schedules and provide schedule-based weather alerts
  • To validate Premium access, handle purchases, restore subscriptions, and support users
  • To control ad serving, reflect consent choices, and keep the service stable
  • To analyze app flows, troubleshoot issues, and improve key features

3. Retention and Deletion

Some settings stored only on the device may be removed when the app is deleted. Account-based data may be retained and deleted in accordance with deletion requests, applicable law, and internal operational policy.

Subscription, billing, and refund records may also be retained under the policies of external providers such as Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following services, each of which processes data under its own policies.

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Analytics, Cloud Messaging, Firestore, and related services
  • Google AdMob and UMP (User Messaging Platform)
  • RevenueCat
  • Apple App Store and Google Play billing systems
  • External data providers such as the Korea Meteorological Administration and the National Transport Information Center

You may request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy-related action regarding your personal data. Requests related to account deletion or stored data may be sent to the contact address below.
