GoodShot Weather

Privacy Policy

Version: v2 · Last updated: March 15, 2026

GoodShot Weather processes only the minimum information necessary to provide sign-in, schedule sync, notifications, subscription features, and core weather services.

1. Information We Collect and Process

  • Account information such as user ID, sign-in provider, and email address when provided through Firebase Authentication
  • App data such as rounding schedules, favorite golf courses, and notification preferences
  • Device and notification information such as push token, platform, and app version for delivering notifications and maintaining the service
  • Subscription status and store purchase identifiers required for validating and restoring Premium subscriptions

2. Why We Process This Information

  • To maintain sign-in state and sync user data across devices
  • To provide schedule-based weather alerts and improve reliability
  • To validate Premium access, remove ads, and restore purchases
  • To respond to support requests and investigate service issues

3. Retention and Deletion

  • Device-only data is removed when the app is deleted from the device.
  • Server-stored account data may be deleted upon account deletion request or according to operational policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may process data under their own privacy policies.

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Messaging, Analytics)
  • Google AdMob
  • RevenueCat and store billing systems such as Apple App Store and Google Play
  • Public Data Portal (Korea Meteorological Administration)
  • National Transport Information Center (ITS)
